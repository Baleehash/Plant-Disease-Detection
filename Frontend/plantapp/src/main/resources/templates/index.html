About Neochloris

Neochloris is an innovative plant analysis tool designed to help farmers and gardeners detect and address plant diseases efficiently. By combining state-of-the-art technology with user-friendly design, Neochloris ensures a healthier and more productive agricultural experience.

Whether you're protecting your crops or nurturing your home garden, Neochloris empowers you with the insights needed to keep your plants thriving.

Disease Library

Explore a curated library of plant diseases to identify and understand the conditions affecting your crops. Gain insights into common symptoms, preventive measures, and solutions for healthier plants.

Apple Scab

A fungal disease affecting apple trees, causing dark spots on leaves and fruits.

Apple Black Rot

A common apple disease caused by fungi, leading to fruit decay and leaf discoloration.

Cedar Apple Rust

A fungal disease requiring junipers and apple trees, causing yellow-orange spots on leaves.
